Priority 1 - Prospect Deep-Dive Prompt Playbook

This document contains copy-and-paste prompt templates designed to help Priority 1 conduct thorough intelligence-driven research on prospective customers. These prompts are structured to uncover a company's products, markets, supply chain, logistics challenges, recent developments, and tailored sales strategies for winning their freight business.

- Full Prospect Intelligence Brief

Do a comprehensive deep dive on Dr. Bronner's as a prospective customer for Priority 1, a freight brokerage offering LTL and full truckload services. Include: overview of products and services; industries and end markets; geographic footprint; freight profile; supply chain structure; logistics challenges; recent news; competitive pressures; Priority 1 value opportunities; and tailored sales angles.

- Products  Freight Profile Translation

Analyze Dr. Bronners' products and translate them into a likely freight and shipping profile. Cover product characteristics affecting shipping, LTL vs FTL mix, palletization and density risk, NMFC/class sensitivity, accessorials, and where Priority 1 can add value.

- Supply Chain & Network Mapping

Map the supply chain of Dr. Bronner's. Include upstream suppliers, manufacturing locations, warehousing, distribution strategy, downstream customers, domestic vs international exposure, and points of transportation risk where Priority 1 can reduce cost or complexity. If sustainability is important to Dr. Bronners' then ensure to include that in the discussion.

- Industry-Specific Logistics Challenges

Based on Dr. Bronners' industry, identify common logistics and transportation challenges such as capacity volatility, rate pressure, service failures, accessorial exposure, claims risk, and visibility issues. Explain how Priority 1's LTL and FTL services address these challenges.

- Recent News & Trigger Events Analysis

Review recent news and press coverage for Dr. Bronner's. Identify acquisitions, expansions, facility changes, leadership shifts, or growth initiatives that may increase shipping complexity and create opportunities for Priority 1.

- Competitive Landscape & Broker Opportunity

Analyze Dr. Bronners' likely logistics provider landscape. Assess asset vs broker usage, common incumbent shortcomings, and where Priority 1's service model can outperform. Provide positioning statements for sales conversations.

- Sales Strategy & Messaging Framework

Create a sales strategy and messaging framework for approaching Dr. Bronner's. Include likely pain points, discovery questions, Priority 1 value propositions, proof points, and objection handling guidance.

- Executive-Level Logistics Summary

Summarize Dr. Bronner's from an executive logistics perspective. Focus on strategic shipping risks, cost drivers, growth impact, and why Priority 1 should be viewed as a strategic logistics partner.

- Customer Analysis

Present all of the analysis as an in-depth internal customer analysis in a .docx file. Keep the formatting of the .docx file clean, modern and organized with headings. Minimal or no use of color. Keep everything clean and modern. Consider use of Inter font. In addition to this customer analysis, at the end of the report include a separate one-page internal deal brief for Dr. Bronner's including company snapshot, freight profile, risks and opportunities, recommended service focus, primary sales angle, and next steps.
